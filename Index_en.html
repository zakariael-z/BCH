About us

BCH Rent A Car is a Belgian car rental company. Founded in 2021 by J.BCH, it has rapidly established itself on the European market. We are proud to offer you a diversified fleet of new vehicles, with an average age of one year, including models from leading brands such as Mercedes-Benz, Audi and Volkswagen. Whether you need a small city car for your city commute, a compact car for your getaway or a spacious station wagon for family trips, we have the vehicle for you. Our commitment is to provide superior service and top-of-the-range vehicles to guarantee your satisfaction. Our branches across Europe offer a wide selection of vehicles to meet all your mobility needs.

BCH Rent A Car offers comprehensive and personalized car rental solutions to make your rental experience as easy and enjoyable as possible. Rely on BCH Rent A Car for your next car rental and discover the excellence of our customer service. Reserve your vehicle today and travel with peace of mind!
